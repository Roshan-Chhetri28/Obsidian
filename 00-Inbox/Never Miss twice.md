The first mistake is never the one that ruins you. It is the spiral of repeated mistakes that follows.
Missing once is an accident missing twice is the start of new mistake.

Author of Atomic Habits states: 

"This is the distinguished feature of winners and losers. Any one can have one bad performance, a bad workout or a bad day.
But when a successful people fail, they rebound quickly.

If you start with 100$ then 50% gain will take you 150 but it only needs 33% loss to take you back to 100 in other words, avoiding 33% loss is as valuable as achieving 50% growth."
